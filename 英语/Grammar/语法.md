
### pick up
For phrasal verbs that can be separated, pronouns (me, her, him, them, it) usually go in the middle. Nouns can go in the middle or at the end.  
可以分开的动词短语，代词通常放中间，名词可以在中间或者后面
  
Pick up the jacket ✅  
Pick the jacket up ✅  
Pick up the jacket = pick the jacket up  
  
Pick it up ✅  
Pick up it ❌
